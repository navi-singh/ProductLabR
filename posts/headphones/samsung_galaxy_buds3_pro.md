## Introduction

The Samsung Galaxy Buds3 Pro represent the most substantial redesign in Samsung's flagship earbud history. Where the Buds2 Pro and Buds Pro used a conventional in-ear bud form, the Buds3 Pro adopt a blade-style wing configuration — a two-part design that places a fin-shaped stabilizer in the outer ear while an ear tip creates the acoustic seal inside the canal. The aesthetic is distinctively Samsung and polarizing by design.

Internally, the upgrade is thorough: Bluetooth 5.4, a dual-driver configuration combining a 10mm woofer and 5.3mm tweeter, IPX7 waterproofing on the earbuds (the strongest in the flagship earbud category), and Samsung Seamless Codec (SSC UHQ) that streams at up to 1 Mbps on compatible Galaxy devices — competitive with or exceeding LDAC at high bitrates.

At $249 — the same price as the AirPods Pro 2 — the Buds3 Pro are Samsung's direct challenge to Apple's flagship earbuds. We tested them over six weeks as daily drivers with a Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra and a non-Samsung Android phone to understand both the full Samsung experience and the limitations outside the ecosystem.

---

## Sound Quality

The dual-driver configuration is the Buds3 Pro's audio signature differentiator. The 10mm woofer handles low frequencies while the 5.3mm tweeter manages mid and high range reproduction. The result is a cleaner, more separated sound than single-driver earbuds produce — bass hits without blurring the mid-range, and treble extends with detail that single-driver designs often sacrifice.

The default sound profile is balanced with a slight low-end emphasis. The Galaxy Wearable app provides a graphic equalizer and preset profiles including Bass Boost, Soft, Dynamic, Clear, and Treble Boost. The equalizer range and preset quality are comparable to Sony's Headphones Connect.

SSC UHQ on a Galaxy device is genuinely impressive. Streaming at up to 1,000 kbps, it delivers more wireless audio bandwidth than LDAC's maximum of 990 kbps — a meaningful specification for audiophiles using Samsung Music or other lossless Android sources. The improvement over AAC is audible on well-recorded hi-res content: improved spatial separation, cleaner transient attack, and more apparent reverb tail in acoustic recordings.

Samsung's 360 Audio with real-time head tracking — available on Galaxy phones and Samsung TVs — adds convincing spatial dimensionality to Dolby Atmos content. For Samsung ecosystem users watching content on a Galaxy phone, the spatial audio experience competes directly with Apple's equivalent.

---

## Noise Cancellation

The intelligent ANC system uses three microphones per earbud in a feedforward/feedback configuration, with real-time analysis of the acoustic seal quality to dynamically adjust the ANC filter. This adaptation means the ANC performs more consistently across different ear canal shapes and eartip fit variations than fixed-filter systems.

In practical performance:
- On metro rail and subway, engine and rail noise attenuated effectively — comparable to the AirPods Pro 2 and a narrow margin behind the Sony WF-1000XM5
- In open offices, HVAC and ambient conversation noise reduced to background texture
- On aircraft, cabin noise was well-suppressed, making the Buds3 Pro a credible travel companion

Third-party measurements place the Buds3 Pro's ANC attenuation at approximately 22–25 dB at mid-to-low frequencies, slightly behind the Sony WF-1000XM5 (estimated 25–28 dB) and AirPods Pro 2 at peak performance, but comfortably ahead of budget competitors.

The Ambient Sound mode passes environmental sound through three microphone inputs per earbud, producing a richer and more natural ambient pass-through than two-microphone designs. Volume adjustment and sound focus (emphasizing voices vs. full ambient) are available in the Galaxy Wearable app.

## Comfort & Design

Fit is the most variable aspect of the Buds3 Pro experience and the area where individual anatomy matters most. The blade-style wing is designed to anchor in the outer ear's antihelix ridge; for users whose anatomy accommodates the blade geometry, the fit is secure and fatigue-free. For users whose outer ear anatomy is incompatible with the wing shape, the Buds3 Pro can feel unstable or mildly uncomfortable over extended sessions.

The ear tip sizes (XS, S, M, L) provide acoustic sealing. The combination of a soft silicone tip and the outer blade anchor creates a different fit mechanism than the stem designs of the AirPods Pro 2. Neither is universally better — they suit different ear shapes. Before purchasing, testing the fit in a retail environment is advisable.

IPX7 waterproofing on the earbuds is the strongest water resistance specification in the premium earbud category. The earbuds can be submerged to 1 meter for up to 30 minutes — suitable for swimming laps, which neither the AirPods Pro 2 (IPX4) nor the Sony WF-1000XM5 (IPX4) can claim. For athletes who train in rain, pool-adjacent environments, or humid climates, this is a meaningful differentiator.

## Features & Connectivity

Galaxy AI integration is the Buds3 Pro's most distinctive functional differentiator. Live Translate, when used with a Samsung Galaxy phone, performs real-time spoken translation — one party speaks in one language, the earbuds translate and play back the translation audibly, and the phone screen shows both the original text and translation simultaneously. In our testing with English/Spanish pairs, the accuracy and latency were impressive for a real-time system.

Interpreter Mode extends this to group scenarios where both participants use the earbuds or phone speaker. For travelers and business users operating in multilingual environments, these features add genuine value that no competing earbud provides.

Multipoint Bluetooth 5.4 handles two simultaneous device connections. SmartThings Find enables location tracking through the Samsung device network, analogous to Apple's Find My — functional and accurate in urban environments.

## Battery Life

Six hours with ANC active and 24 hours in the case totaling 30 hours matches the AirPods Pro 2 specification exactly and represents strong performance for the category. Real-world testing at moderate volume with ANC active consistently achieved 5.5 to 6.5 hours on the earbuds.

The 5-minute quick charge delivering one hour of playback is on par with the AirPods Pro 2. Qi wireless charging on the case is a convenience that several competing cases at this price point still omit.

---

## Who Should Buy It?

- **Samsung Galaxy smartphone users** who want maximum codec performance (SSC UHQ), Galaxy AI features, and seamless SmartThings integration
- **Swimmers and active users** who need IPX7 waterproofing — this is the only flagship ANC earbud suitable for pool use
- **Audiophiles on Android** who want the highest wireless audio bandwidth available on compatible Samsung devices
- **Travelers who want real-time translation** built into their earbuds without a separate app or device

Not recommended for iPhone users (full features unavailable), users with ear anatomy incompatible with the blade-style fit, or listeners who prefer the simplicity of single-driver in-ear designs.

---

## Verdict

The Samsung Galaxy Buds3 Pro are the best ANC earbuds available for Samsung Galaxy users, delivering dual-driver sound quality, SSC UHQ wireless audio, class-leading IPX7 waterproofing, and Galaxy AI features that have no equivalent in competing earbuds. The blade-style fit is fit-dependent and requires testing before committing. Outside the Samsung ecosystem, the case for the Buds3 Pro weakens considerably. At $249, they are a compelling buy for their intended audience.
